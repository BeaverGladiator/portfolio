The Long Island Gasoline Retailers Association is a non-profit trade association, recognized across New York State and locally as aggressive advocates for the small, independent business owners who comprise its membership. Retailers, service station owners and auto repair shop owners have been able to solve many of their operational challenges with LIGRA'S help. Any eligible business owner who joins LIGRA will find his/her savings in overhead costs will more than justify the dues each member pays.

LIGRA also offers a bevy of health insurance plans that have vetted according to whether they work well for small business owners. LIGRA members who get their health insurance through the association also benefit from the services provided by LIGRA's knowledgeable health insurance broker as well as the services of a health services advocate provided free of charge.
