About Us

We have outlined below some important reasons to become a LIGRA member

  • A Legal Plan that makes a high-powered attorney available, to you, in all matters that affect your station or repair shop, including free representation before the Department of Motor Vehicles when you are cited for an infraction.
  • Comprehensive Health Insurance Plans. Click here to view your options.
  • LIGRA maintains a staff at LIGRA Headquarters, available to service our membership, Monday through Friday, 8:30 a.m. to 5 p.m. LIGRA also maintains an answering system that is monitored at all times when the office is closed, in case of an emergency at your station or repair shop.
  • LIGRA publishes a monthly Bulletin that is chockfull of updated Industry news so that you can be informed of developments that will impact your business and industry.
  • LIGRA employs Field Representatives who help members meet their challenges. Each Field Representative is equipped is just a telephone call away when a problem arises. Problems they can help you with include:licensing questions, problems with your gasoline sales representative, or anything that creates a problem with respect to your station or repair shop.
  • LIGRA's Board of Directors is comprised of service station dealers and repair shop owners just like yourself. They are available if you want to talk to another dealer or owner about a problem that you might have concerning your business.
  • LIGRA sponsors clinics and other educational seminars throughout the year in order to help mechanics increase their educational skills, as new and highly technological changes are introduced into our Industry. Such clinics touch on the subjects of how to conduct a proper inspection, the latest techniques in air conditioning repair, brake repair, computer command analysis, rack and pinion steering and the like...LIGRA stresses the importance of education because of the changing nature of the industry.
